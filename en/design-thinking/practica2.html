Design Thinking Workshop

In this second part of the Design Thinking methodology we will cover the second objective:
Develop empathy with users, by observing them. Therefore, it is a methodology based on observing the user.

Design of the web page and approach of the Design Thinking Project

  • The Design Thinking project proposal is human oriented, feasible and functional. And they are involved.
  • The proposal is of high social, economic impact.
  • The work team has the skills to develop the project
  • The proposal is based on the reality and opportunities for innovation within the region
  • Develop a Gantt Diagram of activities for the development of design thinking.
  • They publish the proposal on the website. Where they detail the previous points.

    Design Thinking Workshop

  • They develop each stage as a team.
  • They end in the established times.
  • They know each of the stages of Design Thinking.
  • They develop Seed Material where they explain the methodology of Design Thinking.
  • They develop the same workshop for their design thinking projects.
  • They publish in the web page the workshop of the desgin Thinking and the video of the seed material explaining the methodology.

    Empathize

  • They correctly use the techniques to empathize.
  • Observe and get involved and demonstrate a broad knowledge of the subject.
  • It collects varied information about the problem.
  • They publish on the website the details of the previous points.

    Define

  • They find the root cause of the problem and generate criteria to evaluate the information and counteract it with the correct use of techniques.
  • They frame the problem in a direct approach.
  • Defines the insight of the user, client or consumer.
  • They publish on the website the details of the previous points.

    Devise

  • It correctly uses the Brainstorming technique, to generate solutions.
  • Use the technique of the 6 hats to find an integral solution evaluated from several points of view.
  • They sketch the solution, where each detail of the sketch responds to a need.
  • They publish on the website the details of the previous points.

    Prototype and Test

  • They develop the functional decomposition of the solution by describing in a meticulous and detailed way. They generate the necessary files for it.
  • They develop a Gantt Diagram to execute the implementation of the project.
  • They make a list of project costs.
  • The project is implemented and / or they develop the high impact campaign (creating an advertising video about the product and sharing on social networks).
  • They publish on the website the details of the previous points.
  • The product or service interacts with the user.
  • Make a receiving mesh of information.
  • Create the necessary environment to use the product or service and observe the behavior of the user.
  • Make improvements to the product or service based on user feedback.
  • They publish on the website the details of the previous points.
