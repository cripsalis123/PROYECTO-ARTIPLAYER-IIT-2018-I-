Devise

The information collected only has utility when analyzed. In this stage all the important data must be filtered to create the conclusions that will guide the work. Next to this, the problems that need solution will be specified.

First Stage: Price of the product

A)Cause :

•Our client as well as a large videogame community can not have physical purchases of their favorite virtual games in this case "Dota 2".

B)Criteria:

•In our environment, there is not much knowledge and use of electronic devices such as cards, microcontrollers or tools such as laser machines to perform the materialization of virtual objects.

•Our client only came to find small things about his favorite game such as: a polo shirt, bracelets, etc.

•Our user, like a lot of gamers (players), prefer decorative objects that can be used in their daily life.

C)Solution:

•Our Artiplayer project focuses on this community of players, being present through lamps with illustration of Dota 2.

Second Stage: increased by 25%

A)Cause:

•Our User wishes that the Artiplayer lamp does not occupy a large amount of space and that it can be easy to attach to the furniture where the computer is located.

•You want colorful lights that do not damage your vision.

B)Solution:

•The size of our lamp was reduced to an acceptable space.

•LED spotlights of cold light were implemented that do not consume much energy to the right color, with an acceptable intensity for vision.

Third Stage: increased by 100%

A)Cause:

•The modality of the game in which this project is emphasized is 5 people, a whole team. This Dota 2 game consists of team strategy, for which our client wants to own 5 lamps for the entire team member.

B)Solution:

•It is only necessary to make a bigger one to get more copies of the Artiplayer lamps with the different logos of known professional equipment, all with the same type of function, to be able to satisfy the needs of the User.
